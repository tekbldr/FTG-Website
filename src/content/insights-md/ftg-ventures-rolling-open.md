FTG Ventures is now open for rolling submissions. We fund, build, and operate at the convergence of money and machine intelligence — and we are looking for founders building the infrastructure of that convergence, from pre-seed to Series A.

## Who we back

We take a small number of deliberate positions rather than spraying cheques, so fit matters more than stage. We are most useful to founders working on:

- **Markets** — trading, liquidity, custody, and settlement infrastructure for digital value.
- **Money** — wallets, payments, stablecoin rails, and the tools that make self-custody safe enough for everyone.
- **Intelligence** — applied and agentic AI, with a particular interest in Arabic-first and region-resident systems.

If your company sits at the seams between these — where value meets intelligence — we especially want to talk.

## What "operator-investor" means for you

We are not a passive cheque. FTG is a studio as much as a fund: we have shipped exchange, wallet, and AI infrastructure ourselves, which means we can help with the parts that are genuinely hard — custody discipline, key security, model orchestration, regulatory posture — not just the parts that fit in a board deck. When we invest, we bring operating capacity and conviction, and we stay close.

We build region-first, to a global standard. If you are building for the Gulf, or building globally from the Gulf, we understand the terrain.

## How it works

Submissions are **rolling** — there is no cohort deadline to wait for. Tell us what you are building, why now, and why you. We read everything, and we aim to be fast and straight with you either way.
