# FTG Website — UI/UX & Scale Research + Recommendations

*Deep, source-verified research (June 2026). 25 sources fetched → 112 claims → 25 adversarially verified (19 confirmed, 6 refuted). Primary sources: a16z, Sequoia, Bessemer, Stripe, Anduril, Affinity. Treat blog-originated guidance as directional; primary-site patterns as reusable.*

---

## Executive summary

**Treat the website as FTG's primary proof device for an operator-investor identity — not a brochure.** This is exactly the deliberate, high-investment design strategy Anduril used to earn "SpaceX-tier" credibility with investors, government buyers, and regulators. For FTG that means three moves:

1. **Lead with the thesis, everywhere.** "Markets · Money · Intelligence — owned as one stack" should parse in the first scroll and be carried *first-pixel-to-last* (a16z's manifesto approach), with clear paths for each audience (investors, talent, press/sovereign, users).
2. **Signal scale with hard, checkable proof** — live operating metrics (Stripe's GDP-counter pattern), a Stripe-style **bento grid of interactive modals** presenting Exx1 / PRVAI / PRV Wallet as one comprehensive stack, and named-index aggregate stats. **Not** logo walls or AUM boasts (both refuted below).
3. **Build an a16z/Sequoia/Bessemer-class Insights hub** (vertical × content-type taxonomy, featured + chronological feed, multi-format media, a named recurring research franchise) to reframe FTG as "a source of ideas, not just capital."

The single biggest **platform** upgrade: relationship-intelligence CRM automation on `/pitch`. The single biggest **risk**: AI-accelerated design without human craft — every gravitas-signaling asset needs hand-finishing.

---

## Part 1 — Identity & design (operator-investor gravitas)

- **Design is a deliberate signaling tool; deepen the dark "ink/spark" system — don't dilute it to generic minimalism.** Anduril's Trae Stephens: invested in design "in order for the company to be viewed with the same sheen as a SpaceX… that's the ad, that's the brand, and that's the product." MENA sovereign-tech skews institutional; FTG's engineered dark aesthetic *is* its SpaceX-sheen lever. *(Fast Company; Yashas Mitta — 3-0)*
- **First-scroll clarity beats flair.** Sites are judged in ~10–20s, aesthetics in ~50ms (NN/g). Within the first scroll a visitor must grasp the focus, who it's for, and the operating point of view. This does *not* conflict with the bold aesthetic — lead with a legible thesis *inside* the engineered look. *(joinamply + NN/g — 3-0)*
- **Carry the thesis pervasively, manifesto-style.** a16z shows a "visible, memorable perspective from first to last pixel"; their manifesto repeats "we believe" 113×. Restate *why owning Markets + Money + Intelligence together compounds* on every page — portfolio, Insights, Careers, Pitch. Reinforce with the **network flywheel** (content → founders → talent/ecosystem → more founders → compounds). *(Edgar Allan; a16z — 3-0; flywheel is a thesis, not a proven ROI model)*
- **Explain the operating model, don't just show logos.** Leaders (AI Fund "venture studio," Sequoia "Arc," Dell Tech Capital's concrete operating support) *add* an explanation of how they build/operate on top of logos. Each FTG pillar should state **what FTG funds, builds, AND operates** — this is the operator-investor differentiator. *(joinamply + primary sites — 3-0)*

## Part 2 — Signaling scale + the Exx1 in-progress flagship

- **Use a Stripe-style "bento" grid of interactive modals** for Exx1 / PRVAI (Diwan OS + PRV Copilot) / PRV Wallet — progressive disclosure conveys an integrated "one stack" without walls of text or forced navigation. **This is the device for featuring Exx1 credibly:** a tile headlines its scale while the modal honestly scopes build status ("in active build"). Adapt — Stripe's bento is one company; FTG's pillars are distinct entities. *(BigGo/Stripe design team — 3-0)*
- **Establish scale with real proof devices / live-ish operating metrics** — animated counters in the existing dark + JetBrains-Mono system (e.g., PRV Wallet chains/tx supported, PRVAI/Diwan usage, Exx1 build milestones). Use *real, checkable* numbers; only label "live" if truly live. *(BigGo/Stripe; Stripe's own $1.9T / ~1.6%-of-global-GDP letter — 3-0)*
- **Do NOT lean on AUM figures, portfolio counts, or exit boasts as the primary proof** (refuted 0-3) — quantitative proof works as **named indices and operating metrics**, not boastful positioning copy.

## Part 3 — The Insights / Content Hub

- **Two-axis taxonomy like a16z:** named **verticals** mirroring FTG's pillars/teams (Markets/Exchange · Applied AI · Self-custody/Money · + a MENA / sovereign-digital-economy theme) **crossed with content TYPES** (Essays/Articles · Investment & Build News · Founder Stories · Podcasts/Media · Research & Reports). Each is a navigable archive. *(a16z live — 3-0)*
- **Featured + chronological feed:** a top band of featured flagship tiles/carousel above a reverse-chronological feed, newest marked **"new"**, with **"load more"** pagination. *(a16z live — 3-0)*
- **Win authority with high-volume, multi-format original content** — articles, news, podcasts, white papers, research ("reads like an online magazine," Bessemer). Authority lever is real but content is *one* of several levers; founders "smell manufactured thought leadership," so quality matters. *(Edgar Allan/Bessemer; Edelman-LinkedIn & Harris Poll thought-leadership ROI — 3-0)*
- **Launch a NAMED, recurring, data-backed research franchise** authored by FTG's team on proprietary data — the highest-authority move (Bessemer "State of the Cloud," anchored on a named index + hard metrics). For FTG: e.g. **"State of the Digital-Asset Stack"** or **"State of MENA Digital-Economy Infrastructure,"** annual, with FTG's own index from Exx1/PRVAI/PRV Wallet activity. Present distinct metrics distinctly (don't couple EV with growth rate). *(Bessemer State of the Cloud — 3-0; larger initiative)*
- **Add a serialized founder-story podcast** around inflection points (Sequoia's "Crucible Moments" model). *(Sequoia — 3-0; larger initiative)*
- **Reusable rich detail-page template** (Crucible Moments modules): primary interview + team voices + "Key Lessons" + photo gallery + chaptered transcript — so each story is multi-format, not a plain blog post. *(Sequoia — 3-0)*

## Part 4 — Platform features (Careers + Pitch)

- **`/pitch` — highest-leverage add: relationship-intelligence CRM automation** (Affinity / 4Degrees model): auto-build founder/company profiles from inbox + calendar (zero manual entry); relationship-strength / warm-intro mapping across the FTG team; pipeline stages + review workflow on top of the existing submission flow. Import the *capability*, not vendors' inflated ROI figures. *(Affinity live — 3-0)*
- **`/careers` ATS** — direct precedent was *not* surfaced by surviving claims; needs its own sourced pass (candidate self-service status, structured interview scorecards, talent-CRM/network capture, programmatic job distribution are the likely candidates).

## Part 5 — Prioritized roadmap

**Quick wins (low effort, high signal)**
- Tighten the home hero so the "one stack" thesis + operator-builder claim parse instantly, with four clear audience paths.
- Add an operating-metrics / proof band (real, labeled numbers) in the dark/mono style.
- Add a one-line "what FTG funds, builds & operates" statement to each pillar.
- Carry the thesis line into `/careers`, `/pitch`, and footer copy.

**Medium initiatives**
- **Insights/Content Hub** (`/insights`) — IA scaffold: vertical × type taxonomy, featured + chronological feed, "new" badges, filters, newsletter capture. *(In progress.)*
- **Bento portfolio grid** with interactive modals (Exx1 leading by scale; honest "in build" status).
- Reusable Insights detail-page template.
- `/pitch` pipeline + review workflow upgrades (toward CRM-grade).

**Larger initiatives**
- Named recurring research franchise + proprietary index.
- Serialized founder-story podcast.
- `/pitch` relationship-intelligence (email/calendar auto-capture + enrichment).

## What NOT to do (refuted in research)

- ❌ AUM / portfolio-count / aggregate-exit boasts as the primary proof device. *(0-3)*
- ❌ "Content is the single credibility device." It's one lever among design gravitas, operating-model explanation, and trust signals. *(0-3)*
- ❌ Portfolio = logo wall / case-study showcase as the top priority. *(0-3)*
- ❌ Assuming "clarity + nav beat aesthetics" — both matter; don't sacrifice the engineered identity. *(0-3)*
